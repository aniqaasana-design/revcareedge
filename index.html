FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, pricing, and process.

What is medical billing? +
Medical billing is the process of submitting and following up on insurance claims to receive payment for healthcare services. It involves translating medical procedures into standardized codes and ensuring proper reimbursement.
How do you handle HIPAA compliance? +
We maintain 100% HIPAA-compliant processes at all times. All staff are HIPAA-trained, and our systems use enterprise-grade encryption and access controls to protect patient data.
How do I get started? +
Simply fill out the quote form above or call us. We'll schedule a free consultation, analyze your current setup, and have you fully onboarded within 48 hours — no disruption to your workflow.
What specialties do you cover? +
We cover 100+ medical and non-medical specialties including cardiology, orthopedics, behavioral health, dermatology, neurology, family medicine, and many more. If you don't see yours, just ask!
How does outsourcing billing save money? +
By outsourcing, you eliminate costs associated with in-house billing staff: salaries, benefits, training, and software. Our clients typically save up to 70% on operating costs while improving collection rates.
Do you handle claim denials? +
Absolutely. Our dedicated denial management team performs continuous, rigorous follow-ups on all denied and pending claims until they are resolved, ensuring maximum recovery of your revenue.
